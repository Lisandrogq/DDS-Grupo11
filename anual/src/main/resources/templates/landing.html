Who we are and what we do.

Fridge Bridge is a community-powered initiative dedicated to ending hunger through shared resources. We manage public refrigerators supported by local collaborators, ensuring surplus food reaches those who need it most. Our network includes legal entities, individuals, and technicians who keep the system running with seamless efficiency. By integrating smart technology like sensor alerts, we maintain optimal refrigerator conditions and respond quickly to any maintenance needs. At Fridge Bridge, we believe everyone deserves access to nutritious food, and we work tirelessly to make this belief a reality.
